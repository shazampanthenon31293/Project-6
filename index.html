To Reorganize the Senate and the House Committees. Also to provide for increased efficiency in the legislative branch of the Government, also for creating a couple of joint committees and establishing their purpouses.

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

________,____

Ms. Lofgren(for herself and Mr. Kilmer, Mr. Johnson of Wisconsin, Mr. Peters of Michigan, Mr. Davis of Illinois, Mr. Graves of Georgia, Ms. Maloney and Mr. Jordan) introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Select Committee on Modernization of Congress, Committee on Oversight and Reform, Committee on House Administration, Senate Committee on Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs; for further consideration

A BILL

A bill to abolish all of the existing congressional committees and subcommittes and then create all new committees and sub-committees and creates new joint-committees and who sits on theme

Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. Short title; table of contents.

(a) Short title.—This Act may be cited as the "Legislative reorginization act of 2021".

(b) Committee.—All congressional committees are hereby abolished
